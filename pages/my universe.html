MY UNIVERSE


"My Universe" is an English and Korean song by British rock band Coldplay and South Korean pop group BTS.

It was released on 24 September 2021.

It debuted at number one on the Billboard Hot 100, becoming BTS' sixth US chart-topper and Coldplay's second after 2008's "Viva la Vida".
